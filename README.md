# Study Time Tracker

A simple, distraction-free desktop application to track your study time.

This app is intentionally minimal. No animations, no gamification, no noise â€” just a reliable way to record how much you study.

---

## Features

- Clean and minimal interface
- Accurate study-time tracking
- Offline-first (uses local SQLite database)
- No ads, no accounts, no internet required
- Designed for serious, focused study

---

## Download

Go to the **Releases** section of this repository and download the latest version of the application.

---

## How It Works

- Enter your starting time when you begin studying
- Enter your stopping time it when you finish
- Hit Save Day button to save the timestamps
- Your data is stored locally on your machine using SQLite

Your study data stays with you.
